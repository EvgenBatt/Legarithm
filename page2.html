Frequently Asked Questions

What licenses exist and what are their differences?

The type of license you need depends on the specifics of your business. In general, licenses can be divided into two main categories: offshore and Offshore. 1. Offshore licenses allow you to work on the international market. Jurisdictions such as Anjouan, Kahnawake are examples of popular offshore licenses. However, be aware that offshore licenses will not be recognized in some countries, such as the USA, France or the UK, and an additional local license will be required to operate in these markets. 2. Offshore licenses are usually required to operate in local markets. Examples of such jurisdictions are Mexico and Great Britain. These licenses allow you to operate directly in the country where they were obtained.

How long does it take to get a license?

It all depends on the jurisdiction and the complexity of the process. On average, it takes from 3 weeks and even up to a year.

What is the cost of obtaining a license?

The cost of obtaining a license depends on the chosen jurisdiction and type of license. These may include application fees, annual license maintenance fees, and additional legal fees.

How to choose the right jurisdiction for licensing?

Choosing the right jurisdiction to obtain a license depends on several key factors: first, determine what services you plan to provide and where exactly you will operate. Evaluate the markets you plan to enter and opportunities for cooperation with other gambling providers. Consider licensing and compliance costs for your chosen jurisdiction. We are ready to help you in all these aspects to choose the optimal jurisdiction for your business.
